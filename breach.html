Breach Check

Security breaches happen all the time. Sometimes, they happen without you

even knowing it. haveibeenpwned.org is a site that will let you enter your

email address and it will check if your info has been a part of any recent

breaches. If you show up in a recent breach, please change your password

or other importan information from the breach immediately

Password Storage

Don't store your passwords on a sticky note or a piece of paper. Even if

you hide them, there is a chance that someone could eventually find them,

and get ahold of all of you passwords. Instead, use a password manager to

store them. They are password protected, and you only have to remember one

master password to access them. To find a password manager, go to the "Privacy

Services" section of the site and go to "Password Managers."
